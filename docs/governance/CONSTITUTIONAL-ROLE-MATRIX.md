# Constitutional Role Matrix

This matrix is informative and derived from the Draft Constitution. If this matrix conflicts with VDP-0000, VDP-0000 remains authoritative.

Before valid initial ratification, no role derives constitutional authority from VDP-0000. Arihant Kaul remains the practical repository owner and project steward under repository reality only.

| Role or state | Phase | Core powers | Prohibitions | Appointment or activation | Removal, reduction, or end | Accountable to |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Constitutional Steward | Founder Stewardship after ratification | Maintain canonical repository; appoint initial Maintainers and Editors; authorize ordinary VDP transitions after gates pass; initiate emergency protective action; propose amendments; initiate institutional transition. | Must not bypass gates, fabricate review, claim AI approval, erase records, grant permanent extra-constitutional authority, accept amendments outside process, or continue unilateral authority after Council activation. | Initially Arihant Kaul only upon valid initial ratification; successor by public succession record before Council activation. | Broad powers end when Constitutional Governance activates; interim replacement is limited to continuity and transition. | Accepted Constitution and inspectable governance records. |
| Maintainer | Founder Stewardship or later phases | Review proposals and implementation; merge authorized changes; maintain assigned areas; participate where eligible; nominate contributors. In Maintainer Governance, eligible Maintainers share ordinary VDP acceptance authority under VDP-0000 thresholds. | No automatic constitutional amendment authority, unilateral acceptance authority, unrestricted repository authority, informal reserved-power delegation, or authority outside assigned scope. | Appointment record by eligible authority for the active phase. | Resignation, inactivity, suspension, or removal record. | Constitution, assigned area records, and active governance authority. |
| VDP Editor | Any active phase | Verify metadata and format; assign or confirm identifiers; check lifecycle gates; coordinate reviews; record authorized transitions; identify process defects. | Must not approve own proposal by editorship, decide technical merit solely as editor, change normative meaning without authorization, or fabricate approval. | Appointment record by eligible authority. | Removal or inactivity record by eligible authority. | Constitution, VDP--001, and active governance authority. |
| Reviewer | Any active phase | Provide scoped evaluation of architecture, security, performance, compatibility, governance, evidence, feasibility, or editorial correctness. | Listing as reviewer does not automatically imply approval; conflicts must not be hidden; external reviewers do not automatically receive constitutional authority. | Review participation or assignment record. | Review scope may end by completion, withdrawal, conflict, or governance record. | Review record and active governance authority. |
| Contributor | Any phase | Author VDPs; submit implementations; report defects; challenge decisions; request review; fork under the license. | No governance authority merely by contribution; no guarantee of merge, appointment, acceptance, confidential access, or immunity from proportionate moderation. | Human participation in project work. | Moderation or restriction only through proportionate process. | Constitution, project records, and applicable community rules. |
| Working Group Lead | Any active phase where chartered | Coordinate chartered scope, deliverables, reporting, and decision process. | Working Group activity or popularity does not create constitutional authority. | Working Group charter. | Charter dissolution, expiry, replacement, or removal record. | Working Group charter and active governance authority. |
| Steering Council Member | Constitutional Governance | Exercise permanent governance authority beneath the Constitution; vote on ordinary and constitutional decisions; handle final ordinary appeals. | Must not override Constitution, ignore conflicts, treat repository ownership as authority, count abstentions as affirmative votes, lower hard minimum votes by recusal, or delegate accountable judgment to AI. | Initial activation requires eligible slate, 21-day review, supermajority Maintainer approval, Steward approval or conflict fallback, and Council Activation Record. | Term expiry, resignation, removal, vacancy procedure, inactivity record, or reduced-Council limits below three members. | Accepted Constitution and public Council decision records. |
| Reduced Council | Constitutional Governance below three active Council members | Preserve records; fill vacancies; take time-limited emergency protective action; maintain essential infrastructure; initiate dormancy or archival review; publish continuity records. | Must not accept ordinary VDPs, ratify amendments, permanently transfer canonical authority, remove contributor protections, appoint itself to new terms, or exercise unrestricted Council authority. | Triggered when active Council membership falls below three. | Reviewed every 30 days; if not restored within 90 days, project enters Limited Continuity or Dormant status with recorded reason and recovery path. | Accepted Constitution and continuity records. |
| Interim Constitutional Steward | Pre-Council continuity only | Preserve continuity and initiate governance transition after Steward unavailability. | Must not become unrestricted founder replacement or permanently expand authority. | Active Maintainers appoint after 90 days unavailability, with two-thirds approval and at least two affirmative votes. | Ends when successor, Steward, eligible Council, or institutional governance is validly established. | Constitution, appointment record, and active Maintainers. |
| Limited Continuity | Pre-Council low-authority continuity state | Repository administrators may perform preservation, security, credential rotation, availability, backup, and archival actions only. | No invented constitutional authority; ordinary VDP acceptance, constitutional amendment, and role appointments pause except restoration of previously recorded access. | Triggered when Steward is unavailable for 90 days, no successor exists, and fewer than two active Maintainers are available; also available after unrecovered Reduced Council operation. | Ends only through recorded Steward return, recorded successor, eligible Interim Steward appointment, valid Council resumption, or legally necessary archival conclusion. | Continuity records and preservation duties. |
| Shared-conflict fallback | Any phase | Preservation, security, legal compliance, and time-limited emergency actions may proceed while independent review is sought. | Non-essential decisions, permanent amendments, and rights reductions pause; conflicted actors must not conceal the shared conflict. | Triggered when all otherwise eligible decision-makers share the same material conflict. | Ends when independent participation is restored or limitation and residual risk are recorded for permitted actions. | Constitution, conflict records, and public limitation records. |

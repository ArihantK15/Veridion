Refund Policy

Last updated: July 2026

14-day refund window

If you are not satisfied with your paid plan within the first 14 days of a new subscription, email us for a full refund.

After 14 days

Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time and will remain active until the end of the current billing period. We do not offer prorated refunds for partial months after the initial 14-day window, since the plan gives you continued access through the period you already paid for.

How to request one

Email arihantkaul@outlook.com with your GitHub organization or account name. Refunds are processed through Lemon Squeezy back to your original payment method.
